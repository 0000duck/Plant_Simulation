Day Start
Enter the day when daylight saving time starts. For the U.S. and the EU, enter 0 for Sunday. 0 designates Sunday, 1 Monday, 2 Tuesday, 3 Wednesday, 4 Thursday, 5 Friday, and 6 Saturday.
