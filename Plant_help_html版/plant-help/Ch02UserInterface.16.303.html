Loading area
The Loading area defines the area of the object onto which the 3D Viewer places mobile objects (MUs). By default, the 3D Viewer uses the bounding box of the selected 3D Viewer object.
You can enter the coordinates along the x-axis, the y-axis, and the z-axis under Base position. You can also enter the Length and the Height of the loading space, and you can click Bounding Box to let the 3D Viewer determine the size of the loading space.
