Date and time format
Select the date and time format for your simulation model. You can select the English 12 hour or 24 hour or the German date and time format.
 
Date and time format
Looks like this
yyyy/mm/dd 12 hour
yyyy/mm/dd 24 hour
dd.mm.yyyy 24 hour
