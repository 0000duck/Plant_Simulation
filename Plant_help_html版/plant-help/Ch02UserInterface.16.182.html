Exiting when Plant Simulation Locks Up
When Plant Simulation locks up during a simulation run, press Ctrl+Shift+Alt for 5 seconds to open the Method Debugger:
•
When Plant Simulation is running in the foreground, hold down the Ctrl+Alt+the left Shift keys for several seconds to open the Debugger while a Method is being executed.
•
When Plant Simulation is not running in the foreground, hold down the Ctrl+Alt+the right Shift keys for several seconds to open the Debugger while a Method is being executed.
Plant Simulation shows a dialog asking if it should terminate and create an emergency copy.
When you click Yes, Plant Simulation safes the model under the name Tecnomatix Plant Simulation.<your login name> to the Temp folder on your hard disk. When you restart the program, Plant Simulation opens a dialog asking whether or not to open this model file.
We recommend to not continue working with the crashed simulation model, but to go back to the last regularly saved version.
As opposed to clicking End Task in the MS Windows Task Manager, this emergency copy contains the last unsaved changes you made.
