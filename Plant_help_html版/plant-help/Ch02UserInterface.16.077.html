Week Start
Enter the week when daylight saving time starts. For the default setting -1 daylight saving time starts on the last Sunday of the month you entered under Month Start.
Enter a number between 1 and 4. Week 1 is the first week including the Day Start you set, week 4 is the last week of the month.
