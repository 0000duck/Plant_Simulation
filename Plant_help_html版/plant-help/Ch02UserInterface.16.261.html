Tube conveyor
For a length-oriented object of type tube conveyor, you can select settings for its Legs and for the tube.
For the tube conveyor the 3D Viewer shows the group box tube instead of Lanes.
You can:
•
Click material and select the material of the tube of the conveyor.
•
Enter the thickness of the material of the tube proper.
To edit extrusion profiles of the selected length-oriented object, click Advanced.
