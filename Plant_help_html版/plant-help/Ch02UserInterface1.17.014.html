Add Common Offset
To add a common offset to the selected anchor point, click this.
•
Enter a value for the position into the text box, or click the spin buttons to add a common offset to the position of the selected anchor point on the x-axis, the y-axis, and the z-axis. The settings for the three axes are independent of each other.
•
Enter the angle of rotation in degrees into the text box.
