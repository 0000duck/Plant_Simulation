If you do not want to show one of the default buttons on the Quick Access Toolbar, click it. This grays this command out again until you click it again in the list. An active command shows the check mark to the left.
Plant Simulation enters the customized settings of the Quick Access Toolbar to the registry key HKEY_CURRENT_USER > Software > Tecnomatix > Tecnomatix Plant Simulation 12.2 > WorkspaceENU. When you delete this key, your customized settings will be deleted.
