Minimize the Ribbon
The command Minimize the Ribbon minimizes the Ribbon Bar in the Plant Simulation program window. It then only shows the titles of the tabs without the buttons for the commands. Instead, you can also click the Minimize button .
To show the ribbon bar again, click the button or select the command again.
