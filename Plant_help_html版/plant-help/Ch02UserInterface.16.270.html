MU side to attach
Select the side on which the MU will be attached to the object.
•
Bottom: The MU will be attached to the object from the bottom, i.e., from the local position (0,0,0). This is the default setting.
•
Top: The MU will be attached to the object from the top, i.e., from the positive z-direction.
•
Front: The MU will be attached to the object from the front, i.e., from the positive x-direction.
•
Back: The MU will be attached to the object from the back, i.e., from the negative x-direction.
•
Right: The MU will be attached to the object from the right hand side, i.e., from the positive y-direction.
•
Left: The MU will be attached to the object from the left hand side, i.e., from the negative y-direction.
This setting applies to 3D objects which have a counterpart in 2D, i.e., the material flow objects for the most part. The 3D Viewer computes the dimension of the MUs using their dimension in 2D.
