Ignore transparent area
To be able to select an object that moves under the transparent area of the icon of an object in front of it, select this.
 
Off
On
Here you cannot select the ParallelProc because the transparent area of the SingleProc, shown in gray, is located in front of it.
Here you can select the ParallelProc because Plant Simulation ignores the transparent area of the SingleProc.
