Customize Quick Access Toolbar

--

You can add commands to the Quick Access Toolbar to suit your modeling needs by clicking Customize Quick Access Toolbar > More Commands.

The title bar of the main program window only provides a fixed width for the Quick Access Toolbar . When you add additional commands, Plant Simulation shows them in a second row below the other commands.

Plant Simulation enters the customized settings of the Quick Access Toolbar to the registry key HKEY_CURRENT_USER > Software> Tecnomatix> Tecnomatix Plant Simulation XX.X > WorkspaceENU. When you delete this key, your customized settings will be deleted as well.
