2D Reference Help > Information Flow Objects > Generator > Dialog Window of the Generator > Tab Times > Stop

Enter the time at which the Generator activates the Interval control for the last time.

As Interval and Duration always appear in pairs, the Duration control may be activated even after the time you entered here. If you do not want any time limit to apply for activating the Interval control, enter 0.

You can also select any of the probability distributions. Then, you have to enter the parameters, which the selected distribution requires.
