hitchFront
2D Reference Help > Mobile Objects > Transporter > Methods of the Transporter > hitchFront

Syntax:

 
MU-path-Transporter-rear.hitchFront

The method hitchFront hitches the front of the active Transporter to the back of the Transporter in front of it. When you do not enter the parameter, Plant Simulation attempts to automatically hitch to the trailer located in front of it. You will use the parameter, when the active trailer is located at the end of a track, which has several succeeding tracks on which trailers are located, which could be hitched up.

Plant Simulation copies the values of the attributes Speed and Backwards of the tractor to the trailers hitched up to it.

The active Transporter can either be one of the trailers or the tractor or a Transporter which is not part of a train yet.

When you hitch up two tugger trains both of which trains have a tractor, Plant Simulation deactivates the attribute IsTractor of the train that is hitching up.

Example:

 
-- @ is the transporter which Collided
-- @.frontMu references the transporter in front
-- hitch the current transporter to the transporter driving in front of it
@.hitchfront
