Recording a Video
Step-by-Step Help > Modeling in 3D > Recording a Video > Recording a Video

You can record a video of the crucial parts of your simulation model. This way, you can pass the video on to anybody or publish it on the Intranet or the Internet, without the person viewing the video having to install Plant Simulation in his computer. The recorder creates an AVI file that any multimedia player can play.

The Video ribbon tab provides all tools for recording and playing the video.
