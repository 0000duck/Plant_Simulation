The objects provide these attributes and methods for working with the user-defined attributes themselves:

asString, AttrType, getStatisticsTable, increment, InheritValue, Name, RandomSeed, and StatisticsActive.

You can define your own attributes on the Tab User-defined. Also compare the Methods for Managing User-defined Attributes.
