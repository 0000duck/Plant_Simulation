How the Worker Finds the Shortest Route
2D Reference Help > Resource Objects > Worker > How the Worker Finds the Shortest Route

The Worker always moves to his destination on the shortest route possible, when he can reach it via FootPaths, WorkerPools and Workplaces or Connectors. This only works, when the Connectors connect a FootPath with a WorkerPool, with another FootPath or with a Workplace. The shortest route mechanism does not take into account any Connectors, which connect two WorkerPools, two Workplaces or a WorkerPool and a Workplace.
