Tab Statistics
2D Reference Help > Resource Objects > LockoutZone > Dialog Window of the LockoutZone > Tab Statistics

Plant Simulation shows the most important statistical data of the LockoutZone on the tab Statistics:

Stopped portion

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the stations were stopped by a LockoutZone in percent.

StatStoppedPortion

Stopped count

Shows how often the LockoutZone stopped the stations.

StatStoppedCount

Stopped durations total time

Shows the total time during which the LockoutZone stopped the processing operations of the assigned stations.

StatStoppedSum

Stopped durations mean time

Shows the meantime during which the LockoutZone stopped the processing operations of the assigned stations.

StatStoppedMu

Stopped durations standard deviation

Shows the standard deviation of the time during which the LockoutZone stopped the processing operations of the assigned stations.

StatStoppedDelta

Stopped intervals mean time

Shows the meantime of the intervals during which the LockoutZone stopped the processing operations of the assigned stations.

StatStoppedIntervalMu

Stopped intervals standard deviation

Shows the standard deviation of the intervals during which the LockoutZone stopped the processing operations of the assigned stations.

StatStoppedIntervalDelta

The Statistics Report shows the Stopped Time of the individual material flow objects.
