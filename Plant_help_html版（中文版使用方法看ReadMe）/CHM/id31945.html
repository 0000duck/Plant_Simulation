Service Statistics -- States -- Time Portions of the Exporters
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Shared Properties of the Material Flow Objects > Statistics of the Material Flow Objects > Statistics Report > Exporter and Worker Statistics > Service Statistics -- States -- Time Portions of the Exporters

This topic provides details about the operating times of the different types of Exporters.

The column

Shows the

Read-only attribute

Set-up Portion

portion of the overall statistics time during which the object was setting-up for the services (Exporters ) weighted with the capacity. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesSetupPortion

Working Portion

portion of the overall statistics time during which the Exporter was working, weighted with the capacity. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesWorkingPortion

Repairing Portion

portion of the overall statistics time during which the Exporter spent for repairs, weighted with the capacity. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesRepairingPortion

Transporting Portion

portion of the overall statistics time during which the Worker was transporting parts from Workplace to Workplace. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesTransportingPortion

En-route to Job

portion of the overall statistics time during which the services of the Worker spent for getting to the Workplace or back to the WorkerPool to get the next job order. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesEnRouteIdlePortion, StatServicesEnRouteToJobPortion

Waiting for Importers

portion of the overall statistics time during which the Exporter spent waiting for an importer, weighted with the capacity. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesWaitingImpPortion

Waiting for MUs

portion of the overall statistics time during which the Exporter spent waiting for an MU at the importer , weighted with the capacity. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesWaitingMUPortion

Failed Portion

portion of the overall statistics time during which the Exporter was failed, weighted with the capacity. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesFailedPortion
