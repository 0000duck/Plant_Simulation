Step Into
2D Reference Help > Information Flow Objects > Method > The Method Window > Method Debugger > Menu Bar of the Method Debugger > Debug Menu > Step Into

The menu command Step Into, or F11, executes one line of the debugged Method at a time. If the line happens to call other methods, Plant Simulation will load this Method into the Debugger as well.

Or you can click on the Toolbar of the Method Debugger .

Step Into and Step Over do not differ if the next line of the source code to be executed does not contain a method call.

The Method Debugger shows the elapsed time in the status bar of when you execute one or several instructions in a Method with the commands Step Over, Step Into, Step Out, or Run to Cursor and when executing the instructions takes 10 milliseconds or longer.
