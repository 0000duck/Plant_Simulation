Statistics of the Material Flow Objects
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Shared Properties of the Material Flow Objects > Statistics of the Material Flow Objects > Statistics of the Material Flow Objects

A typical task of a simulation run is to find out the distribution of failure times, waiting times or blockages, and the material flow through the processing stations. When interpreting the simulation run, you can utilize statistical data the material flow objects collect by default.

As a rule, you will always try to arrive at the best configuration of the production process: short throughput time, making the best use possible of the resources available and meeting all deadlines. The standard statistics of objects in Plant Simulation provide data to enable you to make valid statements about the process you are studying.
