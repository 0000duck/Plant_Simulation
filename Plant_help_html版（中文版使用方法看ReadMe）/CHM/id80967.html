Tab Times
2D Reference Help > Information Flow Objects > Generator > Dialog Window of the Generator > Tab Times > Tab Times

Enter a constant time or a probability distribution for the Start time, the Stop time, the Interval, and the Duration of the instructions which you programmed in the Interval control and the Duration control. Some settings require a probability distribution and additional parameters. Click the drop-down list to select the type of distribution, click into the text box to the right of it to show the sequence of the parameters at the top of the tab and then enter the parameters for the distribution you selected.
