Backwards
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Conveyor > Dialog Window of the Conveyor > Tab Attributes > Backwards

To make the Conveyor move in reverse against the direction of the material flow, select this. Note that not only the Conveyor itself, but also the MUs moving on the Conveyor, can move forward and in reverse. This means that a part may enter the Conveyor at its exit and leave it at its entrance.

Suppose you selected Acceleration and an MU on the Conveyor moves forward with a certain speed, with the check box Backwards cleared. When you then select Backwards, the Conveyor, which might represent a belt conveyor, slows down until its speed is 0. Then it accelerates in reverse direction, until it reaches its final speed. When you clear Acceleration , the Conveyor attempts to reverse its direction of motion immediately without decelerating and accelerating again.
