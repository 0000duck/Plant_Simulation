Times and Distributions
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Shared Properties of the Material Flow Objects > Times and Distributions > Times and Distributions

Plant Simulation is a discrete simulation system. It is based on a variety of times, such as processing times, machine down times, set-up times, recovery times, etc. It allows true to life modeling with fixed times as well as with statistically distributed times.
