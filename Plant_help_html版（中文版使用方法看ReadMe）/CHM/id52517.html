To open the Method Debugger for all methods and formulas, which are executed while the event is processed, click . This means that the debugger opens for all executed controls, formulas and for suspended methods, which were woken up. Holding down Shift while clicking Single Step Simulation accomplishes the same, in the EventDebugger and in the EventController.
