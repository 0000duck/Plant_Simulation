MTTR
2D Reference Help > Tools > TransferStation > Dialog Window of the TransferStation > Tab Failures > MTTR

Enter the Mean Time To Repair (MTTR) of the TransferStation into the text box. The mean time to repair is a measure of maintainability. It is the average time required to repair it.

You can enter the MTTR here or on the tab Failures of the ParallelStation. Plant Simulation propagates any changes you make to the respective other object.
