Pipe
2D Reference Help > Fluid Objects > Pipe > Pipe

Pipes transport free-flowing materials between the objects FluidSource , Tank , Mixer , Portioner , DePortioner, and FluidDrain . While material is flowing through the Pipe, it shows the color of that material as its color. This is the color which you selected for the material in the MaterialsTable. You have to establish connections between Pipes and fluid objects with the Connector . To make materials flow between the fluid objects, you have to use Pipes.

To add the Pipe to your simulation model, click Manage Class Library > Basic Objects > Fluids > Pipe on the Home ribbon tab.
