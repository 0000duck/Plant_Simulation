Rail conveyor
2D Reference Help > The Plant Simulation Program Window > Ribbon Bar > Home Ribbon Tab > Edit 3D Properties > Tab Appearance of the Length-oriented Objects > Configuration > Conveyor type > Rail conveyor

For a length-oriented object of type rail conveyor, you can enter its base height, its width. In addition, you can select settings for its Legs, for its rail bars and for the Frame around it.

For the rail conveyor 3D shows the group box rail bars instead of Lanes.

You can:

  • Click material and select the material of the rail bars of the conveyor.

  • Enter the count, i.e., the number of rail bars you want to insert.

  • Enter the width of the rail bars, i.e. their distance from each other.

  • Select if you also want to insert crossbars for additional stability by activating the check box.

    • Click material and select the material of the crossbars that stabilize the rail bars of the rail conveyor.

    • Enter the distance of the crossbars that stabilize the rail bars against each other.
