Tab Suspended
2D Reference Help > Information Flow Objects > Method > The Method Window > Method Debugger > Watch Window > Tab Suspended

The tab shows methods that were suspended by The Waituntil-Statement and the Stopuntil-Statement.

The tab Suspended also shows methods, which have been suspended with a wait-instruction or with a sleep-instruction. The third column shows the statement which suspended the Method.

The caller of the Method is displayed in parenthesis, preceded by the absolute path. Double-click an entry to change to that Method.
