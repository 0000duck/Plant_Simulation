Our StorageCrane has a length of 20 meters and a width of 10 meters. The portal positions are located at 0 meters and 8 meters, meaning that the gantry juts out 2 meter over its columns. This is where the train is going to move.

We distribute 20 times 10 storage places across the store. Each storage place is 1 meter by 1 meter.

We mark rows 9 and 10 as barred area in which the tracks of the train run. Other than that, we do not change any of the default settings of the crane.
