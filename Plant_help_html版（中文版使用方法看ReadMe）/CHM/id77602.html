Portioner
2D Reference Help > Fluid Objects > Portioner > Portioner

The Portioner portions the produced products and creates mobile objects out of them. You can then connect the Portioner with one of the Material Flow Objects to further process the created MUs.

You can think of the Portioner as the counterpart of the DePortioner.

To add the Portioner to your simulation model, click Manage Class Library > Basic Objects > Fluids > Portioner on the Home ribbon tab.
