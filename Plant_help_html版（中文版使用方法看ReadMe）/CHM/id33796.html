Slower/Faster
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > EventController > Dialog Window of the EventController > Tab Controls > Slower/Faster

To increase the speed of the simulation, drag the slider to the right or press the right arrow key. To decrease the speed of the simulation, drag it to the left or press the left arrow key.

Decreasing the speed enables you to better follow the movements of MUs, as Plant Simulation shows their icons for a longer time on the same station.
