Behavior of Blocked Objects
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Shared Properties of the Material Flow Objects > Behavior of Blocked Objects

A material flow object, whose capacity is exhausted, will reject any MUs attempting to move onto it. It then enters these MUs into its Forward Blocking List, its Backward Blocking List, or its Exit Blocking List. The first MU entered in the list will also be the first to be processed after the blockage is removed. The EventController informs the first MU in the list, when a processed MU exits a material flow object and it thus is ready to receive the next one.
