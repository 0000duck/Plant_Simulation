Tab Statistics
2D Reference Help > Tools > CrossSlidingCar > Dialog Window of the CrossSlidingCar > Tab Statistics

The tab Statistics shows these statistics values.

The left column shows the basic statistics values. The right column shows the values which are specific to the CrossSlidingCar.

Item

Shows the

Working

portion of the statistics collection period during which the cross-sliding car was working.

Waiting

portion of the statistics collection period during which the cross-sliding car was waiting.

Blocked

portion of the statistics collection period during which the cross-sliding car was Blocked.

Failed

portion of the statistics collection period during which the cross-sliding car was Failed.

Paused

portion of the statistics collection period during which the cross-sliding car was Paused.

Unplanned

portion of the statistics collection period during which the cross-sliding car was Unplanned.

Waiting

portion of the statistics collection period during which the Vehicle of the cross-sliding car was waiting for the next transport order.

Driving

portion of the statistics collection period during which the Vehicle of the cross-sliding car was driving to a loading point.

Loading

portion of the statistics collection period during which the Vehicle of the cross-sliding car was being loaded.

Transporting

portion of the statistics collection period during which the Vehicle of the cross-sliding car was driving to a unloading point.

Unloading

portion of the statistics collection period during which the Vehicle of the cross-sliding car was being unloaded.

Damping

portion of the statistics collection period during which the Vehicle of the cross-sliding car was in the damping time.
