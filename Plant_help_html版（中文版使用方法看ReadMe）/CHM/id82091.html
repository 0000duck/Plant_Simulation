Speed
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Conveyor > Dialog Window of the Conveyor > Tab Attributes > Speed

Enter the final transport speed of the MUs on the Conveyor. You can also enter -1 for an infinite speed.

  • When you change the speed, the program automatically recalculates the Time, and vice versa, provided you deactivated the feature Acceleration. The processing time is the Length divided by the speed.

  • When you activate acceleration, the Conveyor attempts to reach the final speed no matter if a part is located on it or not. A part entering the Conveyor moves with the actual speed of the Conveyor. When the Conveyor currently accelerates, the part will accelerate as well.
