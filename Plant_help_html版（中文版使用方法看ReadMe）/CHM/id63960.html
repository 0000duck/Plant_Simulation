Tab Statistics
2D Reference Help > Resource Objects > Worker > Dialog Window of the Worker > Tab Statistics > Tab Statistics

Plant Simulation shows the most important statistical data on the tab Statistics.

It shows the values for the Services and for the Worker. For each of these blocks, adding up the individual values results in 100 percent.

The Worker collects failed times of the services and of the Worker.

The Worker only collects Failed times that accrue during the processing time, i.e., times that are located outside of the paused and of the unplanned time.

Waiting times only accumulate during the times, when the Worker is available, i.e., when these times are located outside of the paused, the unplanned, and of the failed times.

Services setting-up

Shows the portion of the set-up time of the services of the overall statistics time of the Worker. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesSetupPortion

Services processing

Shows the portion of the working time of the services of the overall statistics time of the Worker. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesWorkingPortion

Services repairing

Shows the portion of the repairing time of the services of the overall statistics time of the Worker. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesRepairingPortion

Services transporting

Shows the portion of the transporting time from Workplace to Workplace of the services of the overall statistics time of the Worker. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned timeandthe paused time.

StatServicesTransportingPortion

Services en-route to job

Shows the portion of the time of the services of the overall statistics of the Worker spent for getting to the Workplace or back to the WorkerPool to get the next job order. The latter only applies when you select Get job orders in the pool only. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesEnRouteToJobPortion

Services waiting

Shows the portion of the waiting time of the services for an importer plus the time the services spent waiting for an MU at the importer of the overall statistics time of the Worker. The value of StatServicesEnRouteIdlePortion is part of the value of StatServicesWaitingImpPortion. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time .

StatServicesWaitingPortion, StatServicesWaitingImpPortion StatServicesWaitingMUPortion

StatServicesEnRouteIdlePortion

Services failed

Shows the portion of the failed time of the services of the overall statistics time of the Worker. The overall statistics time is the statistics collection period without the unplanned time and the paused time.

StatServicesFailedPortion

Exporter operational

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the Worker was operational.

StatExporterOperationalPortion

Exporter paused

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the Worker was paused.

StatExporterPausedPortion

Exporter unplanned

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the Worker was unplanned.

StatExporterUnplannedPortion

Exporter failed

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the Worker was failed.

StatExporterFailedPortion

Worker traveled distance

Shows the distance which the instance of the Worker traveled from the Workerpool to the Workplacesattached to the stations and between the stations in meters.

StatTraveledDistance

To collect statistics data, select the check box Exporter statistics.

To view Exporter and Worker Statistics in the Statistics Report, click the object in the Frame, and press F6 (Show Statistics Report), or click Show Statistics Report on the Home ribbon tab.

You can also click the object in the Frame with the right mouse button and select Show Statistics Report on the context menu.
