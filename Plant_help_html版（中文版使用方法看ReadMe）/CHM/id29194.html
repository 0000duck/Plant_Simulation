Driving Statistics of the Transporter
2D Reference Help > Mobile Objects > Shared Properties of the Mobile Objects > Statistics of the Mobile Objects > Driving Statistics of the Transporter > Driving Statistics of the Transporter

In addition to product and resource statistics, the Transporter collects driving statistics data about the states Order occupied, Order empty, Home driving, Ready, Failed, Paused, Unplanned, the battery consumption and the number of jobs performed.

To view Driving Statistics of the Transporter in the Statistics Report, click the object in the Frame, and press F6 (Show Statistics Report), or click Show Statistics Report on the Home ribbon tab.
