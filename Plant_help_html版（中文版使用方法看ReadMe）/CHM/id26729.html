Structure Pane of the Display Window
2D Reference Help > Display and User Interface Objects > HtmlReport > Dialog Window of the HtmlReport > Show Report > Structure Pane of the Display Window

Proceed as follows to execute the described actions in the structure pane of the display window:

  • To jump to a certain heading of level 1 or 2 in the display window, click on this heading in the structure pane.

  • To print the report, click the button.

  • To show a print preview of the report, click .

  • To save the contents of the HTML page as a Web Page, complete, i.e., as an .htm-file or an .html file, click .

  • To update the displayed data with the current data of the model, click .

  • To scroll the contents of the structure pane and of the display pane up, roll the mouse wheel forwards.

  • To scroll the contents of the structure pane and of the display pane down, and roll the mouse wheel backwards.

  • To zoom the contents of the structure pane and of the display pane in, hold down the Ctrl key and roll the mouse wheel forwards.

  • To zoom the contents of the structure pane and of the display pane out, hold down the Ctrl key and roll the mouse wheel backwards.
