Texture
3D Reference Help > 3D Reference > Edit Ribbon Tab > Insert Textured Plate > Texture

Define the settings for the texture here.

  • To show the texture on both sides of the plate, i.e., on the top and the bottom or on the front and the back, select Show on both sides.

  • To fit the picture of the texture to the size of the plate, select Fit texture to plate. Note that, depending on the dimensions of your picture you enter under Plate, the texture might be being distorted.

  • To activate tiling, select Tiling.

  • Enter the width of the individual tiles.

  • Enter the height of the individual tiles.
