Video compression
3D Reference Help > 3D Reference > Video Ribbon Tab > Video Settings > Video compression

3D shows the current codec (coder/decoder, compression/decompression) in the drop-down list. The codec translates the video between its uncompressed form and the compressed form in which it is stored.

  • Select a codec from the drop-down list Video Compression.

  • Click Apply and/or OK to apply the Video Settings you selected.

3D adds additional codecs you install on your computer to the drop-down list Video Compression.

Check the World Wide Web for information about the different kinds of codecs available.
