2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > AssemblyStation > Dialog Window of the AssemblyStation > Tab Attributes > Assembly table > Open

To open the assembly table, click this. Depending on the setting you selected from the drop-down list, you either enter the numbers of the Predecessors or the MU types into the cells of column 1 and their amount into the cells of column 2.

Enter the number of the predecessor or the type of MU-types for the assembled parts into the table, not for the main MU!
