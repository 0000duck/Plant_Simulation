Customize the Behavior of Objects
Step-by-Step Help > Modeling in 2D > Modeling the Flow of Materials, Advanced > Customize the Behavior of Objects > Customize the Behavior of Objects

You can customize the behavior of most of the Plant Simulation objects to meet your modeling needs.

  • You can program and assign control methods to an object. The object then reacts to certain user actions, such as inserting and deleting objects, etc.

  • You can define user-defined attributes for an object, which it does not provide by default.
