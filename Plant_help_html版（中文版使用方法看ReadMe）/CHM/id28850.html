Parts from
2D Reference Help > Tools > TransferStation > Dialog Window of the TransferStation > Tab Attributes > Parts from

Enter from which station the TransferStation gets the MUs. The MUs can also be located on a means of transport on the parts station. You can also drag that object, i.e., the parts station, over the icon of the TransferStation and drop it there.

The parts station can be any one of these material flow objects: Source, Conveyor, TwoLaneTrack, Track, ParallelStation, Station, AssemblyStation, DismantleStation, Buffer, and PlaceBuffer.

A TransferStation, which you inserted into a Frame, shows the station type, the part station, and the target station in the Tooltip.
