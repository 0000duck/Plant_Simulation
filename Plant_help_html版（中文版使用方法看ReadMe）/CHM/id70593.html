Tab Statistics
2D Reference Help > Fluid Objects > Mixer > Dialog Window of the Mixer > Tab Statistics

In addition to the values described under the Tab Statistics the tab Statistics of the Mixer shows this object-specific value.

Total throughput

Shows the amount of product that flowed through the Mixer.

StatThroughput

Relatively empty

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the Mixer was Empty in relation to the time during which it was available.

StatRelativeEmptyPortion
