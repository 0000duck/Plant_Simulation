Attach a Camera to an Object
Step-by-Step Help > Modeling in 3D > Modeling a Fly Through > Attach a Camera to an Object

To attach a camera, through the lens of which you look at the scene, to an object, proceed as follows:

  • Select a single object.

  • Click on the View ribbon tab of the 3D window.

  • 3D attaches the camera at a defined location:

    3D uses the center of the top of the bounding box of the selected object as the location where it inserts the camera. Note that 3D adds an offset of 0,0,0.1 to these makeshift points, so that your eyes will be located above the top plane.

    By default the object camera faces on the positive x-axis using the positive z-axis as Up direction.

As soon as the object, with a camera attached to it, moves through the scene, the camera moves along with it automatically.

Once the camera is attached, you see the scene through the lens of this camera.
