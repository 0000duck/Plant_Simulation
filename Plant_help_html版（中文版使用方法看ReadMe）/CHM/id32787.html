Resource Statistics of the MUs
2D Reference Help > Mobile Objects > Shared Properties of the Mobile Objects > Statistics of the Mobile Objects > Resource Statistics of the MUs

As the Transporter and the Container can load and transport other MUs, they provide Resource Statistics

The resources statistics of the MUs only provide the Material Flow Properties.

To view Resource Statistics of Moving Resources in the Statistics Report, click the object in the Frame, and press F6 (Show Statistics Report), or click Show Statistics Report on the Home ribbon tab.
