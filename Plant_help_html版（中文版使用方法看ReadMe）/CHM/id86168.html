Step-by-Step Help > Animating the Simulation Model and Viewing the Results > Working with Evaluation Tools > EnergyAnalyzer > Tab Evaluate > Show

To show a chart of the energy consumption of the selected objects, click Show.

Note that the chart of the EnergyAnalyzer show the energy consumption in kilowatt hours (kWH). The built-in Energy Statistics chart, on the other hand, shows the energy consumption in percent of 100.

The colors for the energy states are identical in both charts.
