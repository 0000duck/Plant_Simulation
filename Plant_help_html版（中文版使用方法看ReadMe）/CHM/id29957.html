Tab Statistics
2D Reference Help > Fluid Objects > Tank > Dialog Window of the Tank > Tab Statistics

In addition to the values described under the Tab Statistics the tab Statistics of the Tank shows this object-specific value.

Total throughput

Shows the amount of material that flowed through the Tank.

StatThroughput

Relative occupation

Shows the sum of all dwelling times of all materials during the statistics collection period during which the Tank was not paused and not failed.

StatRelativeOccupation

Relatively empty

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the Tank was Empty in relation to the time during which the Tank was available.

StatRelativeEmptyPortion

Relatively full

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the Tank was full in relation to the time during which the Tank was available.

StatRelativeFullPortion
