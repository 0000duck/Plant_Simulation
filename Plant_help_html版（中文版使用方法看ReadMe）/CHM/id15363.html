Drain
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Drain > Drain

The Drain removes the parts and workpieces, which the Source produced, from the plant after they have been processed. You can use it to model the shipping department of your plant.

The built-in properties of the Drain are the same as those of the Station. Like it, it has a single processing station. The only difference is that the Drain removes the processed part from the plant instead of moving it on to a succeeding object in the flow of materials.

You can also show the drain statistics table of a Drain in an HtmlReport, compare Display a Drain.

To add the Drain to your simulation model, click Manage Class Library > Basic Objects > MaterialFlow > Drain on the Home ribbon tab.

Compare the sample models: Click the Window ribbon tab, click Start Page > Getting Started > Example Models and click Small Examples. Then, select the respective Category, the Topic, and the Example in the dialog Examples Collection and click Open Model.
