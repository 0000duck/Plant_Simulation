Tab Type Statistics
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Drain > Dialog Window of the Drain > Tab Type Statistics > Tab Type Statistics

On the tab Type Statistics you can select to collect statistics for MUs with the same name that pass through your simulation model by activating Type dependent statistics .

The tab shows this statistical data:

Working

Shows the percentage of the sum of the times during which the MUs were located on a working object, in relation to the statistics collection periods of all MUs. In general, the statistics collection period is the life time of the MUs.

StatStoreWorkingPortion+ StatProdWorkingPortion+ StatTranspWorkingPortion

Setting-up

Shows the percentage of the times during which the objects were setting-Up for the MUs, in relation to the statistics collection periods of all MUs.

StatStoreSetupPortion+StatProdSetUpPortion+StatTranspSetUpPortion

Waiting

Shows the percentage of the times during which the objects were waiting for the MUs, in relation to the statistics collection periods of all MUs. In general, the statistics collection period is the life time of the MUs.

StatStoreWaitingPortion+StatProdWaitingPortion+StatTranspWaitingPortion

Stopped

Shows the percentage of the sum of the times during which the MUs were located on an object that was stopped, in relation to the statistics collection periods of all MUs. In general, the statistics collection period is the life time of the MUs.

StatStoreStoppedPortion+StatProdStoppedPortion+StatTranspStoppedPortion

Failed

Shows the percentage of the sum of the times during which the MUs were located on an object that was failed, in relation to the statistics collection periods of all MUs. In general, the statistics collection period is the life time of the MUs.

StatStoreFailPortion+StatProdFailPortion+StatTranspFailPortion

Paused

Shows the percentage of the sum of the times during which the MUs were located on a paused or unplanned object, in relation to the statistics collection periods of all MUs. In general, the statistics collection period is the life time of the MUs.

StatStorePausingPortion+StatProdPausingPortion+StatTranspPausingPortion

Average lifespan

Shows the average life-span of MUs, which were created and removed from the plant during the statistics collection period. Only those MUs are counted for which you activated product statistics.

StatAvgLifeSpan

Average exit interval

Shows the average time interval between exits of the MUs that the Drain removed from the plant. It results from adding up the times of all intervals, and then dividing them by the number of intervals. Statistics only starts counting from the time the first part arrived!

StatAvgExitInterval

Total throughput

Shows the number of MUs that the Drain removed from the plant, starting at the time, at which you activated Type dependent statistics.

StatDeleted

Throughput per minute

Shows the number of MUs that the Drain removed from the plant in a minute over all observed times during which the Drain was available.

StatThroughputPerMinute

Throughput per hour

Shows the number of MUs that the Drain removed from the plant in an hour over all observed times during which the Drain was available.

StatThroughputPerHour

Throughput per day

Shows the number of MUs that the Drain removed from the plant in a day over all observed times during which the Drain was available. This is the value of the throughput per hour multiplied with 24.

StatThroughputPerDay

The sum of the percentages for the states working, setting-up, failed and paused of the MUs adds up to 100 percent.

To view the Part Types which the Drains Removed from the Plant in the Statistics Report, click the object in the Frame, and press F6 (Show Statistics Report), or click Show Statistics Report on the Home ribbon tab.

You can also click the object in the Frame with the right mouse button and select Show Statistics Report on the context menu.
