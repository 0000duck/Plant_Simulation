Video Settings
3D Reference Help > 3D Reference > Video Ribbon Tab > Video Settings > Video Settings

The command Show Connections provides settings for recording and saving the AVI file.

In the dialog Video Settings you can enter the Frame rate, the Duration, the Record client area, the Output file, and select the type of Video compression.

  • To apply your changes to the settings and leave the dialog window open, click Apply.

  • To apply your changes to the settings and close the dialog window, click OK.

  • To close the dialog without applying any of the settings you modified, click Cancel.
