Amount

Shows how many MU instances of this class existed during the statistics collection period. The program only counts those MUs whose product statistics was active during the simulation run. The Containers may be located in different Frame hierarchies.

StatCount

Deleted

Shows how many MU instances of this class were removed from the plant during the statistics collection period. The program only counts those Containers whose product statistics was active during the simulation run.

StatDeleted

Average lifespan

Shows the average life-span of the MUs, which were created and removed from the plant during the statistics collection period. The program only counts those Containers whose product statistics was active during the simulation run.

StatAvgLifeSpan

Product Statistics is described under the Tab Product Statistics. The tab itself shows these values.

To view Product Statistics of the MUs in the Statistics Report, click the object in the Frame, and press F6 (Show Statistics Report), or click Show Statistics Report on the Home ribbon tab.
