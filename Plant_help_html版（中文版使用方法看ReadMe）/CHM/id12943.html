Width
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Converter > Dialog Window of the Converter > Tab Attributes > Width

Enter the width of the Converter. Plant Simulation inserts the Converter with the standard width of 1 meter.

When the Converter is to convey parts perpendicular, it has to provide enough room for the length and the width of the MU to completely fit on the Converter, when the booking point is located on the change over point in the center of the Converter.

You can only enter the Curve width in pixels when you deactivate Transfer length. When you activate it, Plant Simulation computes the width in meters.
