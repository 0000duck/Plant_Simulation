Built-in Attributes and Methods for the Appearance of the MUs in 3D
SimTalk Reference > 3D-related SimTalk Functions > Built-in Attributes and Methods for the Appearance of the MUs in 3D > Built-in Attributes and Methods for the Appearance of the MUs in 3D

Plant Simulation provides these attributes for automatic graphics of the MUs in 3D:

_3D.ActivateMaterialWithColor, _3D.AutoGraphicsActive, _3D.MaterialActive, _3D.MaterialAmbientColor, _3D.MaterialDiffuseColor, _3D.MaterialEmissiveColor, _3D.MaterialShininess, _3D.MaterialSpecularColor, and _3D.MaterialTransparency.
