Simultaneously Reactivating Several Methods with waituntil
SimTalk Reference > General SimTalk Functions > Control Flow Statements > Suspending Methods > The Waituntil-Statement and the Stopuntil-Statement > Reactivating Methods > Simultaneously Reactivating Several Methods with waituntil

Transporters are to move from several objects to the same Station. Remember that a Station can only accept an MU, if it is Empty. We programmed the method of the exit controls of the predecessor objects like this: 

waituntil Station.empty prio @.capacity
@.move(Station)

Suppose we have three suspended exit controls. Once the Station is empty, the Transporter with the highest capacity moves to the Station, as it has the highest priority. After the Transporter moves to the Station, the condition Station.empty is false. Thus, the remaining two Transporters stay where they are and wait until the Station is empty again.
