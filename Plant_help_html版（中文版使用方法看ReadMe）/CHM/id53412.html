Plant Simulation Gantt Chart
Setting-Up and Starting > Plant Simulation Options > Plant Simulation Gantt Chart

Plant Simulation GanttChart is a program for visualizing Gantt charts, for example production workflows.

The GanttChart can visualize data from a simulation run. In addition to the mere occupancy data, the GanttChart can also display failures of machines, pausing times, and blocking times.

This way the GanttChart immediately shows which machines or stations are overloaded or have a low utilization and which production orders or products have long waiting times during the production process.
