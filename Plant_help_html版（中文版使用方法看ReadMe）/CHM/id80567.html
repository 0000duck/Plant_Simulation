Station type
2D Reference Help > Tools > TransferStation > Dialog Window of the TransferStation > Tab Attributes > Station type

Select what you want the TransferStation to accomplish below Station type . Depending on your selection, the TransferStation activates or deactivates options on the Tab Advanced Attributes.

  • Load parts. Here the TransferStation gets the parts from the parts station and loads them onto a means of transport ( Container or Transporter ), which has to be located on the target station. Loading starts as soon as part and means of transport are ready.

    To load the parts onto a means of transport, select this radio button.

  • Unload parts. Here the TransferStation unloads the parts from a means of transport ( Container or Transporter ), which is located on the parts station, and places them onto the target station. Unloading starts as soon as a means of transport is ready at the parts station.

    To unload the means of transport, select this radio button.

  • Reload parts. Here the TransferStation unloads the parts from a means of transport ( Container or Transporter ), which is located on the parts station, and places them onto a means of transport ( Container or Transporter ), which is located on the target station. Reloading starts as soon as both means of transport are ready.

    To reload parts, select this radio button.

  • Move parts. Here the TransferStation unloads the parts from the parts station and loads them onto the target station. Transferring starts as soon as a part on the parts station is ready.

    A TransferStation, which you inserted into a Frame , shows the station type , the part station , and the target station in the Tooltip.
