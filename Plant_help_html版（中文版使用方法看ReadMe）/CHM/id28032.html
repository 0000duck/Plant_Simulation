Pauses
2D Reference Help > Resource Objects > ShiftCalendar > Dialog Window of the ShiftCalendar > Tab Shift Times > Pauses

Enter the times of the breaks for each shift into these cells. Enter the hour and the minute when the break starts, a hyphen and the hour and minute the break ends. When you define several breaks, separate them with a semicolon.

To define a coffee break from 9 o’clock to a quarter past 9 o’clock, and a lunch break from 12 o’clock to a quarter till 1 o’clock, enter 9:00-9:15;12:00-12:45.

To make the ShiftCalendar check if the values you entered for the breaks are plausible or not and if you used the correct format to enter the break times, click Apply.
