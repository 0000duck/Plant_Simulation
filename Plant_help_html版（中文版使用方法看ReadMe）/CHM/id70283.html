Automatically Place Parts into Stock, Remove Parts from Stock on Demand
Step-by-Step Help > Modeling in 2D > Modeling Transport Systems > Modeling a Transport System with Passive Objects of Type Track > Model a Facility Using a Storage Crane > Automatically Place Parts into Stock, Remove Parts from Stock on Demand

To model a portal crane, which automatically places parts into stock and which removes parts from stock on demand, proceed like this:

  • Insert the StorageCrane into your simulation model. Connect it with its predecessor.

  • Double-click the StorageCrane to open its dialog window. Define its properties, such as its size, the number of storage places, etc.

  • The successor has to ask the crane to remove parts from stock. For this you will employ the method getPart. In our example the crane removes 4 parts of type Part with the priority 20 from the store.

    The source code looks like this:

    @.move
   if self.~.empty 
      PortalCrane.getPart("Part", 4,20)
end

  • Insert an EventController into the Frame, start the simulation and watch the crane place parts into stock and remove them from stock.

  • Click the tab Statistics and check how many parts the crane placed into storage and how many it removed from storage.
