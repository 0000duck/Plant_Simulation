Configure the ShiftCalendar
Step-by-Step Help > Modeling in 2D > Simulating the Power Consumption in Your Facility > Configure the ShiftCalendar

To model a realistic situation in the factory, we insert a ShiftCalendar that controls the hours during which people and machines work in the factory. We did not change the shift times, we just entered the material flow objects.

To do so, we held down the Shift key and dragged a marquee over the objects. We then dragged the selected objects onto the ShiftCalendar and dropped them there.
