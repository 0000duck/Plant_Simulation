Mouse wheel behavior
2D Reference Help > The Plant Simulation Program Window > Ribbon Bar > File Menu > Preferences > 3D > Mouse wheel behavior

Select the mouse wheel behavior, i.e., what 3D moves when you roll the mouse wheel in the scene.

  • Move camera moves the camera away from you when roll the mouse wheel down. When you roll the mouse wheel up, 3D moves the camera towards you.

  • Move scene moves the scene as such towards you when you roll the mouse wheel down. When you roll the mouse wheel up, it moves the scene away from you. This is the default setting.
