Legend
2D Reference Help > Display and User Interface Objects > Chart > Dialog Window of the Chart > Tab Labels > Legend

Select if and on which side of the Chart window the program shows the legend for the values the Chart displays.

  • Off, i.e., no legend.

  • On the Right hand side, in one line.

  • On the Right hand side, in two lines.

  • On the Right hand side, stacked. This setting shows the input channels from bottom to top instead of from top to bottom. It is especially well suited for the Chart types > Stacked Columns and 100 % Stacked Area.

  • On the Left hand side, in one line.

  • On the Left hand side, in two lines.

  • At the Top, in one line.

  • At the Top, in two lines.

  • At the Bottom, in one line.

  • At the Bottom, in two lines.
