Length
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > TwoLaneTrack > Dialog Window of the TwoLaneTrack > Tab Attributes > Lane A > Length

Enter the Length of lane A of the TwoLaneTrack. After you inserted it, Plant Simulation shows its length here. The length of the lanes of the TwoLaneTrack and the sum of the lengths of all Transporters located on it determine how many Transporters it can accommodate at any one time.

Each lane of the TwoLaneTrack may have its own length to realistically model the length of the lanes, when the TwoLaneTrack turns a corner. In that case the outside lane is longer than the inside lane.

When Transfer length is active, the program adapts the graphic, when you change the length of lane A of a TwoLaneTrack, which only consists of a single straight segment. When the length cannot be exactly displayed in pixels, the program adapts the length of the graphic. If you need to use the exact length, clear transfer length.

You can only enter the Length of lane A, when you deactivate transfer length or when the TwoLaneTrack consists of a single straight segment.
