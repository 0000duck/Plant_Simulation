Unplanned
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Shared Properties of the Material Flow Objects > Statistics of the Material Flow Objects > Resource Statistics > Unplanned

A material flow resource is unplanned, when you did not enter a time in the ShiftCalendar during which it is going to work.

Plant Simulation only increments the counter for pauses and unplanned times when the duration of the pause or shift pause is not 0.

The LED of an unplanned station is light blue .
