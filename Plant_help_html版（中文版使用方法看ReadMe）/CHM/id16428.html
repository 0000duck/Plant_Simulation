Statistics of the Mobile Objects
2D Reference Help > Mobile Objects > Shared Properties of the Mobile Objects > Statistics of the Mobile Objects > Statistics of the Mobile Objects

When interpreting simulation runs, you can call upon the statistical data that the MUs collect automatically. The statistical data may refer to a single MU only or to all MUs of the MU class collecting data.

The material flow objects usually process the mobile objects (MUs) according to an operating plan. During their live time they are located on production, transport and storage objects. If the objects they are located on are not failed or paused, mobile objects are either being processed or are waiting. The Plant Simulation standard statistics for MUs provides data for making valid statements about the distribution of these different states.
