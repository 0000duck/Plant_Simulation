How It Works and What You Can Do with It

The ExperimentManager supports you in executing simulation studies. A simulation study consists of several experiments, which the ExperimentManager automatically executes. You will carry out simulation studies to investigate different variants of the input values and arrive at statistically safe results

You will typically carry out your simulation studies at times when you do not need your Plant Simulation license and your computer for your daily work. This will usually be at night.

The ExperimentManager can also distribute the simulation runs onto several different computers within your local network, compare Executing Distributed Simulations. For executing the simulation studies you need one of these Plant Simulation license for each of the used computers: Professional, Standard, Application, Runtime, Research, or Educational. For controlling the simulation study the ExperimentManager needs one of these licenses: Professional, Application, Research, or Educational .
