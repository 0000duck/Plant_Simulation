Capacity
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > TwoLaneTrack > Dialog Window of the TwoLaneTrack > Tab Attributes > Capacity

Enter the Capacity of the TwoLaneTrack into this text box. The capacity is the maximum number of Transporters that may be located on both lanes TwoLaneTrack as a whole or in part at any one time. Enter -1 for an infinite capacity.
