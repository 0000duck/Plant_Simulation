Tab Statistics
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > AssemblyStation > Dialog Window of the AssemblyStation > Tab Statistics AssemblyStation > Tab Statistics

Statistics is described under the Tab Statistics. In addition, the AssemblyStation collects these statistical values:

Waiting parts

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the AssemblyStation was Waiting for mounting parts.

StatWaitingPartsPortion

Waiting resources

Shows the portion of the statistics collection period during which the AssemblyStation was waiting for Exporters and/or mounting parts.

StatWaitingResPortion

To view the Waiting Times for Parts in the Statistics Report, click the object in the Frame, and press F6 (Show Statistics Report), or click Show Statistics Report on the Home ribbon tab.

To open the Waiting Times Table, which shows the sum of the waiting times for mounting parts for each predecessor, click this.

You can also click the object in the Frame with the right mouse button and select Show Statistics Report on the context menu.
