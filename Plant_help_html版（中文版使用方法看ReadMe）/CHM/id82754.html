Length
2D Reference Help > Material Flow Objects > Converter > Dialog Window of the Converter > Tab Attributes > Length

Enter the length of the Converter. After you inserted it, Plant Simulation shows its length here. When the part is conveyed straight through along the direction of insertion, its length and the length of the parts determine how many parts it can accommodate.

When the Converter is to convey parts perpendicular, it has to provide enough room for the length and the width of the MU to completely fit on the Converter, when the booking point is located on the change over point in the center of the Converter.
