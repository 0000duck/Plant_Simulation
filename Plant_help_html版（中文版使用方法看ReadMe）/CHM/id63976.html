This topic provides details about battery-related values of the Transporters.

The column

Shows the

Read-only attribute

Battery Capacity

maximum charge capacity of the battery. The charge cannot exceed this value.

BatCapacity

Charging Current

charge current of the battery.

BatChargeCurrent

Number of Charges

number of times the battery was charged.

StatBatChargeCount

Portion

portion of its life-span during which the battery of the Transporter was charging.

StatBatChargePortion

Driving Consumption

battery consumption while the Transporter is moving.

BatDriveCons

Basic Consumption

basic energy consumption that comes off the battery. This applies as long as the Transporter is running under battery power.

BatBasicCons
