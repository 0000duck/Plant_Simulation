Controlling Your View
Step-by-Step Help > Modeling in 3D > Controlling Your View > Controlling Your View

3D provides several ways of setting how you look at the 3D scene, i.e., of controlling the camera that represents the eyes of the viewer looking at the scene.

You can:

  • Set the Main Directions

  • Set View Points

  • Attach a Camera to an Frame and Detach it

  • Animate the Camera
