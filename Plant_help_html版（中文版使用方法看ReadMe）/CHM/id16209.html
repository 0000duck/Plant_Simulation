Tab Product Statistics
2D Reference Help > Mobile Objects > Shared Properties of the Mobile Objects > Dialog Window of the Mobile Objects > Tab Product Statistics > Tab Product Statistics

The tab Product Statistics provides the dialog item Product statistics.

To view Product Statistics of the MUs in the Statistics Report, click the object in the Frame, and press F6 (Show Statistics Report), or click Show Statistics Report on the Home ribbon tab.
