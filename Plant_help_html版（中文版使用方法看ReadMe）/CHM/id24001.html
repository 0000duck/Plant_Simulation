Frame rate
3D Reference Help > 3D Reference > Video Ribbon Tab > Video Settings > Frame rate

Accept the default frame rate for recording a video or enter a frame rate of your choice into the text box Recording rate.

3D shows the actual recording frame rate in the field Actual, which will be less than the desired frame rate if the recorder cannot keep up with that frame rate.

Accept the default playback rate or enter a frame rate of your choice into the text box Playback rate. The higher the playback rate, the faster the video plays.
