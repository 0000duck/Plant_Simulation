Efficiency
2D Reference Help > Resource Objects > Worker > Dialog Window of the Worker > Tab Attributes > Efficiency

Enter the efficiency in percent with which the Worker works. It determines how fast the Worker performs the task assigned to him:

  • With an efficiency of 100 percent he consumes the exact processing time, which you entered.

  • With an efficiency of 200 percent he consumes half of the processing time, which you entered.

  • With an efficiency of 50 percent he consumes twice the processing time, which you entered.

When more than one Worker performs jobs at the station, the efficiency of the slowest Worker determines the processing time of the part/MU.
